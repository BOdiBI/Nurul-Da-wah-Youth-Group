About Us

What We Are

NURUL UMMAH DA'WAH YOUTH GROUP is a community-based charity organization dedicated to improving the lives of economically and socially disadvantaged individuals in the Mt. Kenya Region. We focus on holistic development through education, empowerment, and community support.

Vision

Our vision is to create a society where every individual has access to essential resources and opportunities, fostering an environment of growth and empowerment.

Mission

Our mission is to uplift and empower youth and underprivileged communities by providing educational resources, basic needs, and community programs that promote self-sufficiency and social welfare.

Goals

  • Provide educational resources and support to enhance learning opportunities.
  • Offer basic needs such as food, clothing, and shelter to the less fortunate.
  • Empower youth through skill development programs and community initiatives.
  • Promote awareness and advocacy for social justice and equality.
  • Encourage community involvement and volunteerism to foster a spirit of giving.
